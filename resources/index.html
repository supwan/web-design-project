Key Organizations

World Food Programme (WFP)

The world's largest humanitarian organization addressing hunger and promoting food security. WFP provides food assistance to millions of people affected by conflict, disasters, and poverty.

Emergency Response School Feeding Nutrition

Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO)

UN agency leading international efforts to defeat hunger. FAO works to improve nutrition, increase agricultural productivity, and build resilient food systems.

Agriculture Food Security Rural Development

International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD)

Specialized UN agency focused on eradicating rural poverty and hunger. IFAD invests in rural people, empowering them to increase food security and improve nutrition.

Rural Development Smallholder Farmers Climate Adaptation
